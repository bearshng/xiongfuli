Biography

I joined the School of computer science and engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology in October 2019, where I am currently an Associate Professor. I am also working as a Macau Young Scholar with the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City, Macau University. My research lies in hyperspectral computer vision and remote sensing image interpretation. I am the Topical Associate Editor of IEEE TGRS and Guest Editor of Remote Sensing and IET Computer Vision Journal. I am a co-recipient of the Best Student Paper Award of DICTA 2020 and the Best Paper Award on Deep Learning for Earth Observation and Remote Sensing@DICTA 2021. I am actively seeking excellent students to work with me on machine learning and computer vision. If you are interested, do not hesitate to contact me via email.
